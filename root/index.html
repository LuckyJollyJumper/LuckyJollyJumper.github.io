HEXED

Physcial 2-6 player boardgame, where players need to explore and gather resources in a hexagonal world by building structures or using magic to gain the most victory points. Each player gets personalised missions to earn extra victory points but, beware of the monsters that lurks in the forests.

Rhythm Quest

A singleplayer roguelike deckbuilder game made in Unity using Fmod aimed at learning the exam material presented in the course Sound and Music Technology at Utrecht University.

Overcooked

A 2 player cooperative game with the goal of making the most pizzas under a certain time limit. Made to analyse and model player behaviour and difficulty of the maps in the game. Made for the course AI for Game technology, Utrecht university.

D&D virtual board
